The inhabit. Ramp

From Day One to Your First Deal Fast

A structured 90-day program that gets agents productive quickly, with ongoing coaching and accountability built in from the start.

01

Motivation & Expectations Meeting

A 1-hour deep dive to align on your 3-year vision, honesty expectations, accountability framework, and offer terms.

02

Orientation & Systems Setup

ICA signing, license transfer, MLS activation, Lofty CRM configuration, and a full platform walkthrough in week one.

03

30-Day — Foundation

Scripts, database-building (200+ contacts), first prospecting calls, and shadowing experienced agents on live appointments.

04

60-Day — Activation

First live unsupervised appointments, offer writing, contract management, and your first transaction start to finish.

05

90-Day — Independence

Pipeline mastery, annual business planning, first closing, and graduating to your full independent model.

06

Ongoing Coaching & Vision Reviews

Weekly team meetings, monthly 1:1 coaching calls, and quarterly vision reviews tied to your personal goals.
