Training Philosophy

We believe agents don't fail because of the market — they fail because of ambiguity. Our program is designed to eliminate guesswork: every agent knows exactly what's expected of them, how they'll be held accountable, what tools they have, and who to call when they're stuck. Skills before software. Mindset before metrics. Accountability above all.

Pillar 01
Skills-Based Foundation
Scripts, role-play, and live shadowing before technology and marketing tools.
Pillar 02
Structured Accountability

Phase 01 Foundation Weeks 1-4 · Days 1-30
Week 1
Orientation & Identity
  • inhabit. vision, values, and "The Team Way"
  • ICA review, commission structure, HR paperwork
  • MLS, lockbox, and association setup
  • Lofty CRM walkthrough — contacts, pipeline, tasks
  • Email, website, and business card setup
Deliverable: Signed 30/60/90 Day Plan
Week 2
Mindset & Motivation
  • 3-Year Vision Exercise (4-quadrant easel method)
  • Personal production goals set and documented
  • Time blocking — the ideal week calendar
  • The 5 Cold Hard Truths about building a business
  • Database-building: first 200 contacts in CRM
Deliverable: Database loaded; ideal week template set
Week 3
Scripts & Prospecting
  • Pre-qualifying scripts (new and experienced agents)
  • Buyer and seller consultation scripts
  • Daily role-play structure (30-60 min/day)
  • Objection handling: top 10 real estate objections
  • Co-op calls and open house networking
Deliverable: First role-play evaluation completed
Week 4
Shadow & Observe
  • Shadow mentor on 2 buyer consultations
  • Shadow mentor on 1 listing appointment
  • Contract walkthrough — as-is and full contract
  • MLS listing entry and compliance training
  • First supervised prospecting calls
Deliverable: 30-Day Review with broker
Phase 02 Activation Weeks 5-8 · Days 31-60
Weeks 5-6
Live Appointments
  • First unsupervised buyer consultation
  • First listing presentation (with feedback debrief)
  • Write first offer — coach reviews before submission
  • Negotiation fundamentals: price, terms, contingencies
  • Lofty behavioral lead scoring deep dive
Deliverable: First live offer written and submitted
Weeks 7-8
Transaction Management
  • Accepted offer to closing — full transaction walkthrough
  • Escrow, title, and lender coordination
  • Inspection response strategy and repair negotiation
  • Commission Disbursement Authorization (CDA) process
  • Client communication cadence during transaction
Deliverable: 60-Day Review — pipeline & activity audit
Weeks 5-8
Marketing & Brand
  • Social media content calendar setup
  • Listing marketing templates (Lofty + Canva)
  • Just Listed / Just Sold campaigns
  • Geographic farming basics
  • Email drip campaigns from CRM
Deliverable: First listing assets published
Weeks 5-8
Tech Mastery
  • Lofty AI lead scoring — prioritizing your pipeline
  • Automated follow-up sequences in CRM
  • ShowingTime optimization
  • DocuSign workflow — offers through commission
  • Market data reporting for clients
Deliverable: CRM pipeline active with 20+ leads
Phase 03 Independence Weeks 9-12 · Days 61-90
Weeks 9-10
Pipeline Mastery
  • Active pipeline management — warm / active / closing
  • Weekly lead review discipline
  • Referral system activation (past clients, sphere)
  • Open house strategy for lead generation
  • Expired and FSBO prospecting (advanced agents)
Deliverable: Pipeline with 5+ active opportunities
Weeks 11-12
Business Planning
  • Annual production goal setting (units and GCI)
  • Reverse-engineering the business math
  • Budget planning: marketing, tools, education
  • Identifying growth path (solo, team, broker)
  • Self-assessment vs. 3-year vision goals
Deliverable: Written Annual Business Plan submitted
Weeks 9-12
Advanced Skills
  • Price reduction conversation scripts
  • Multiple offer strategy for buyers
  • Working with investors — ARV basics
  • Relocation and referral network activation
  • Commercial real estate fundamentals
Deliverable: 90-Day Graduate Review with broker
Day 90
Graduation & Path Forward
  • Final 90-day performance review
  • Production metrics vs. plan review
  • Path confirmed: Solo Agent / Team / Broker
  • Ongoing coaching schedule established
  • Team presentation: "What I learned in 90 days"
Deliverable: Official graduation + next path plan

Note for Broker / Manager: This program should be customized based on each agent's experience level. New licensees (0-18 months) should follow the full program as written. Experienced agents (18+ months) may fast-track Weeks 1-4 but must still complete all legal documentation, a full technology setup, and a tailored 30-day plan. Abbreviated onboarding is never an excuse to skip documentation. Review and update this program annually or whenever major policy, technology, or regulatory changes occur.
